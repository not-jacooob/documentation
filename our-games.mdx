<Accordion title="Portbay Retail" defaultOpen icon="shopping-bag" iconType="solid">
  Portbay Retail was Volare Studios first and currently only publicly announced project. Portbay Retail was first created on the 26th May 2025 before Volare Studios was even founded. Portbay Retail is a retail group based on popular store chains in the UK such as Marks & Spencers, Lidl and Asda. Portbay Retail has currently been in development for close to a year now and still is where our small development team of just 2 people are contstantly working on it day to day where possible in their busy life.
</Accordion>
