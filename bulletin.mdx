<Accordion title="Volare Studios has temporarily ceased game Operations." icon="warning">
  <sup>Published on the 15/04/2026 @ 09:26 PM BST</sup>

  This is due to the new Roblox Publishing Requirements and Evaluations processes (more information about this can be found [**here**](https://devforum.roblox.com/t/new-publishing-requirements-evaluation-process-for-games/4573166)) making our games accessible to all age groups is no longer possible due to the new Roblox Plus subscription requirement. We do understand that this is a small cost however we currently do not make any profit of our games and nor does our team have enough funds to invest in this currently. This temporary cease of game operations is only temporary and also applies to our other communities such as Portbay Retail, however if we are unable to find a solution this make become permanent. Thank you for your understanding, and we hope to find a solution soon.
</Accordion>
