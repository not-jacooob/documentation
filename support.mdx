## Our Departments

Here are all of our all of our current departments at Volare Studios.

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Column>
    <Card title="Project Management Department" icon="hammer">
      The project management department is responsible for the planning and coordination of our games and communities.
    </Card>

    <Card title="Support Department" icon="phone-rotary">
      The support department answers all inquiries weather that is from emails to the support form or even updating our websites FAQ.
    </Card>

    <Card title="Staffing Department" icon="people-group">
      The staffing department is responsible for each staff members employment here at volare studios. The also recruit new staff when required.
    </Card>
  </Column>
  <Column>
    <Card title="Quality Assurance Department" icon="book-user">
      Our quality assurance department ensures our games are being made to our standards as well as debugging each game before release.
    </Card>

    <Card title="Internal Affairs Department" icon="book-user">
      The internal affairs department ensures our staff are abiding by our rules and meeting all their requirements their position requires.
    </Card>

    <Card title="Training Department" icon="graduation-cap">
      The training department is responsible for creating each departments training program and training all new staff to our standards.
    </Card>
  </Column>
</Columns>

<Card title="Public Relations Department" icon="handshake" horizontal>
  The public relations department handles all the social media, marketing and partnership aspects of Volare Studios.
</Card>
